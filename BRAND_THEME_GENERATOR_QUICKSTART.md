## â“ FAQs

### Q: Does this replace the sidebar uploader?
**A:** No! Both work together. Use this for **global consistency**, use sidebar for **per-widget customization**.

### Q: Can I use this with any widget?
**A:** Yes! All 8 widgets automatically receive your brand theme.

### Q: What if I don't like the generated presets?
**A:** You can still manually customize colors in each widget's sidebar.

### Q: Does my theme get saved?
**A:** Yes! It's saved in your browser's localStorage and persists across sessions.

### Q: Can I share my theme?
**A:** Yes! Click "Export Theme" to download a JSON file you can share.

### Q: What if I upload a new logo?
**A:** It will override your current theme. You can export the old one first as a backup.
